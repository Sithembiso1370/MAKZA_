Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get approved?
Most seller applications are reviewed within 24-48 hours. Once approved, you can immediately start listing products and making sales.
When do I receive payment for my sales?
Payments are released to your bank account 3-5 business days after the order is delivered and confirmed by the buyer. You can track all payments in your seller dashboard.
Can I sell internationally?
Yes! Makzaz operates in over 50 countries. You can choose which countries you want to ship to when setting up your listings. We provide international shipping labels and customs documentation support.
What if a customer wants to return an item?
Sellers must accept returns for items that are damaged, defective, or not as described. You can set your own return window (minimum 14 days). Return shipping costs depend on the reason for return.
Is there a limit to how many products I can list?
No! There's no limit on the number of products you can list. New sellers can start with as few or as many listings as they want. We recommend starting with 10-20 quality listings to test the market.
How do I handle customer service?
As a seller, you're responsible for responding to customer inquiries about your products. We provide a messaging system within the platform. Aim to respond within 24 hours to maintain good seller ratings.
