Our Story

Founded in 2024, Makzaz began with a simple vision: to create a global marketplace where anyone, anywhere can buy and sell with confidence. The name "Makzaz" represents our commitment to making commerce accessible, secure, and enjoyable for everyone.

From our humble beginnings to becoming a trusted platform serving millions of customers across continents, we've stayed true to our core belief that commerce should bring people together, not divide them.

Today, Makzaz operates in over 50 countries, connecting buyers with sellers, supporting local businesses, and creating opportunities for entrepreneurs worldwide.
