Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MAKZAZ

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Enter your shipping details and payment information to complete your order.

What are the delivery times?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is available for 1-2 business days. Free shipping on orders over R500.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package in real-time.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact our support team to initiate a return.

How do I become a seller on MAKZAZ?

Contact our team at sellers@makzaz.com with details about your products. We'll guide you through the verification and onboarding process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and cash on delivery for select areas. All payments are secured with encryption.
