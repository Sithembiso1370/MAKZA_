Frequently Asked Questions

What items are eligible for return?
Most items in new condition with original packaging can be returned within 30 days. Some exceptions apply: final sale items, personalized products, perishable goods, and intimate apparel cannot be returned.
How long does it take to get my refund?
Once we receive your returned item, it takes 2-3 business days to inspect and process. After approval, refunds are issued to your original payment method within 5-7 business days depending on your bank.
Who pays for return shipping?
For defective, damaged, or incorrect items, we cover all shipping costs. For returns due to change of mind or sizing issues, a flat shipping fee may be deducted from your refund.
Can I exchange an item instead?
Yes! You can request an exchange for the same item in a different size or color. Select "Exchange" as your return reason and we'll send the replacement once we receive the original item.
